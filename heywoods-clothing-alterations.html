What We Do

Alterations for Everyday Clothes, Suits and More

Proper workmanship on all kinds of garments. If you are not sure, just call and ask.

Trouser Shortening

Trousers taken up to the right length with a clean, professional finish. Hemmed by hand or machine depending on the fabric.

Jeans Alterations

Jeans shortened with the original hem kept intact when required. Waist adjustments and tapered legs also available.

Waist Adjustments

Waist taken in or let out on trousers, skirts and dresses. We check the fit properly to make sure it sits right.

Suit Alterations

Jackets and trousers adjusted for a better fit. Sleeves, shoulders, waist and length all altered with care.

Jacket & Blazer Work

Blazers and casual jackets altered to fit properly. Length, sleeve and waist adjustments done with proper tailoring.

Coat Repairs

Coats repaired and altered including linings, buttons and seams. Winter coats, overcoats and lighter styles.

Zip Replacements

Zips replaced on jeans, trousers, jackets, dresses, bags and more. We match the colour and type where possible.

Dress & Skirt Alterations

Dresses and skirts taken in, let out or shortened. Lined and unlined garments both handled with care.

Clothing Repairs

Torn seams, split hems, loose buttons and general wear repaired. We will tell you honestly if it is worth fixing.

Curtain Alterations

Curtains shortened, lined and adjusted. Also roman blinds and other soft furnishings altered to fit.

Our Story

A Family Trade Since 1977

Heywood's Clothing Alterations has been part of Blackpool's alteration trade since 1977. Jack grew up around tailoring and garment making, with his mother running her own tailoring and clothing alteration shop.

He later took over the business and built years of experience altering clothes for the public, local businesses, clothing shops, sports shops and visitors to Blackpool.

The business is now based in South Shore and focuses on proper workmanship, honest advice and personal service.
