Commercial Takeaway

Volatility Reality

Energy market volatility is not theoretical; it is immediate, sustained, and commercially consequential. The 17% weekly Brent rally, 20x war-risk insurance increase, and physical crude at $150/bbl while futures trade at $107 are direct cost drivers.

The period of manageable energy costs has ended. The question is not whether prices will rise, but how much, how fast, and whether mitigation is possible.
