Commercial Takeaway

Strategic insights for procurement decision-making

Summer Procurement Opportunity

Priority

The Summer '26 strip at £85.40/MWh represents fair value. Consider layering in 30-50% of anticipated summer load over the next 4-6 weeks before storage injection demand lifts gas prices.

Winter Risk Premium Justified

Priority

The £28/MWh winter premium reflects genuine supply risk. Don't expect significant softening unless EU storage fills exceptionally fast. Lock core winter demand by September.

Carbon Price Support

EUAs holding above €65/t provide structural support to power prices. The Market Stability Reserve continues to tighten supply. Factor carbon escalation into multi-year contracts.

Flexibility Value Rising

With day-ahead volatility at 12% (30-day realized), contracts with volume flexibility carry significant value. Consider take-or-pay structures vs fixed volume for uncertain loads.

Warning Signs

Emerging risks and potential market dislocations to monitor

Nordic Hydro Reserve Depletion

Critical

Norwegian reservoir levels at 52% vs 68% seasonal average. If dry weather persists, Nordic power exports to Continental Europe could be severely constrained by August, lifting UK interconnector imports.

Asia LNG Demand Recovery

High

Japanese LNG spot buying up 18% YoY. JKM-TTF spread narrowing to $0.80/MMBTu — lowest since 2023. European cargoes may divert to Asia if premium widens.

French Nuclear Maintenance Schedule

High

EDF Q3 maintenance schedule heavier than expected with 12GW offline in July (vs 8GW planned). France likely net power importer through summer, increasing UK export obligations via IFA.

UK Capacity Market T-4 Results

Medium

T-4 auction cleared at £65/kW/year — above expectations. New build CCGTs and battery storage dominant. Existing coal and diesel units retiring as planned.

Grid Balancing Costs Elevated

Medium

BSUoS charges averaging £8.20/MWh this month (vs £5.80 seasonal norm). Constraint management in Scotland and intermittency costs driving system operator actions higher.
