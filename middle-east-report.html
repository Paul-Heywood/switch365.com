Executive Summary

The U.S.-Iran ceasefire, brokered through Pakistani mediation on April 7, 2026, establishes a fixed 14-day duration commencing April 8, 2026. This temporal framework, explicitly confirmed by both parties and validated across multiple authoritative sources, results in a verified expiration date of April 22, 2026.

Key Findings:

  • Confirmed Duration: 14 days explicitly stated in founding agreement [13] [14] [32]
  • Calculated Expiration: April 22, 2026 validated across U.S., Chinese, Malaysian, and Turkish media [35] [76] [83]
  • Extension Status: No formal extension confirmed as of April 16, 2026 [19] [29] [41]
  • Official Denials: Both U.S. and Iranian officials rejected extension speculation [38] [77]
