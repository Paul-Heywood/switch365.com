Impact on UK Business Energy Prices

Gas Price Transmission Mechanism

UK NBP as TTF-Derived Market

The UK National Balancing Point is fundamentally a TTF-derived market with 0.85–0.95 correlation, adjusted for basis differences and physical interconnection capacity.

Physical links: Bacton-Zeebrugge Interconnector (20 bcm/year), BBL pipeline (16 bcm/year) - both functioning with tight basis spreads

European Gas Dependence on LNG Marginal Pricing

European gas pricing is set at the margin by LNG delivered cost, with LNG imports comprising ~45% of European supply and rising.

Domestic production: ~30%, cost-plus
Pipeline imports: ~25%, contracted
LNG imports: ~45%, marginal price setter
